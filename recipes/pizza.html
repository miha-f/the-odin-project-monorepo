This is the best homemade pizza recipe, inspired by traditional Italian methods. It features a crispy crust, rich tomato sauce, and delicious toppings.

You will need the following ingredients

  • 500g all-purpose flour
  • 300ml warm water
  • 7g dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 200g tomato sauce
  • 200g mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Your favorite toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, olives, etc.)

Follow this instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix warm water, yeast, and salt. Let sit for 10 minutes.
  2. Gradually add flour and knead into a smooth dough. Let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat oven to 250°C (480°F).
  4. Roll out the dough and place it on a baking tray.
  5. Spread tomato sauce, add cheese, and top with your favorite toppings.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Garnish with fresh basil and enjoy!
