This is the best recipe for Lasagna, told to my by my Nonna. It follows the traditional Italian lasagna recipe.

You will need the following ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Minced meat
  • One large white onion
  • Two large carrots
  • Three celery sticks
  • Salt and pepper
  • Tomato puree/sauce
  • Cheese

Follow this instructions

  1. Cut onion, carrots, celery into small cubes
  2. Cook your meat in the pan (until nicely browned)
  3. Add veggies (onion, carrots, celery)
  4. Cook for few minutes, until onions are translucent
  5. Season to taste
  6. Add tomato purree/sauce
  7. Add 200ml of water
  8. Simmer until nice sauce is created
  9. Build lasagna (add pasta, meat sauce, cheese, repeat)
  10. Top of lasagna should be covered with cheese
  11. Bake until golden brown
  12. Enjoy
